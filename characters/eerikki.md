# Eerikki

The center. Not the hero in the traditional sense — the condition everything
else happens around. Ancient, technically. Has been alive long enough that
the void's no-time has made centuries feel like sequence rather than duration.
Worldborn originally — came from a planet, which nobody who matters does anymore.

He caused the disruption. Honestly didn't mean to. Genuinely trying to fix it.
Has the intelligence, the resources, and the centuries to pursue the fix at
scale. Is making it worse for everyone except himself.

His ship has a forest. It also has an ocean. He put them there because he
couldn't stop being from somewhere that had those things. The grief is real.
The engineering is extraordinary. Both are true simultaneously.
