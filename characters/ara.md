# Ara

Dead. Has been dead a long time. Speaks anyway — in the structural gaps,
between chapters, in Eerikki's memory.

Knew the routes before they were routes. Watched the diaspora's
infrastructure get installed. Knows what Eerikki's program is actually
doing, from the outside. Cannot finish another person's song.
Has been watching him try.

Her name: Eerikki knows it. Nobody else does. The reader never learns it.
