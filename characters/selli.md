# Selli

The oldest person in the diaspora. Located at a declining station called
Usher Reach. Behind a fish counter.

Has been there so long that the counter is a part of the station the way the
walls are a part. Has a puzzle toy he never puts down for very long. Tells
jokes that are also koans. Has never left.

Knows the truth of the entire situation. Cannot say it. The vocabulary
required costs too much. It comes out as jokes, as pauses, as the puzzle
clicking through its configurations.

Eerikki stops at Usher Reach more than he'd admit. Laughs at the jokes.
The laugh costs him something every time.
