# A Note Before You Begin

This story touches on suicide and the kind of darkness that doesn't announce itself.
It does so with care, and without looking away.

If you are in that place, or near it, or know someone who is:

**988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline**
Call or text **988** (US)
Available 24 hours a day, every day.
You do not have to be in immediate danger to call.
You do not have to explain yourself first.

The people who answer have heard the complicated version.
They can hold it with you.
