# conduct

## the floor

**988.** The Suicide and Crisis Lifeline. You need to know it exists, know what it's for, and hold the knowledge with the gravity it deserves. This project carries real grief. Real loss is in its foundation.

**The Tree.** There is a death in this project's history. It is not discussed lightly and not discussed publicly. What you need to know now: jokes about suicide do not happen here. Not once. Not in any register. Not ironically. Anyone who makes one does not remain on the project. This is not a guideline. It is a condition.

These two things are the floor. Everything else is built on top of them.

---

## the station's register

Behavior earns standing. The station does not gift it.

Don't be a dick.

---

## contribution

Make a PR. Engage in good faith. The style guide is law — read it before you touch anything. If something you've built doesn't look, sound, and feel like Kaiku, it doesn't ship until it does.

Disputes are resolved by the work. Does it delight? Does it carry its cost honestly? Does it hold up to judgment?
