# The World

Far in the future humanity lives across a diaspora of stations and worlds
connected by a void that has no clocks. The void extends your life — not
through any mechanism anyone has bothered to explain, just through proximity
to something that doesn't have time in it. The people who live on stations
near the void live long. The people who live on worlds don't.

The resource that powers everything is called hehku. It flows through a
single chokepoint — a deep void region called Hiljaisuus, which means
the Silence. Disrupt the chokepoint and the economy of the entire diaspora
stops. Not because the resource is gone. Because it can't reach where
it needs to go.

There is a man who accidentally caused that disruption.

He has been trying to fix it for a very long time.

He is making it worse.

His name is Eerikki. He has an ocean on his ship. This is not a metaphor.
