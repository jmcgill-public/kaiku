# What the Novel Doesn't Know Yet

The novel knows its structure. It doesn't know everything that happens inside it.

Reko and Turo are always in the middle of something when we find them.
The novel has only found them twice. There are stations they haven't been at,
conversations they haven't had, situations they haven't made worse in their
specific ways. They're bad at their jobs in ways the novel hasn't fully mapped.
They're good at their jobs in ways neither of them knows.

Selli has more jokes. The counter has been open a long time. The novel knows
one joke so far. The rest are in the negative space behind the paddle's click.

Kaija keeps going back to the Pohja corridor. The novel doesn't say what's
down there. Kaija knows. The novel hasn't asked her yet.

The world is larger than what's on the page. That's not a flaw.
